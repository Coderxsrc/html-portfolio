My top 3 movies of all time

Boy this is a difficult one because I am sure there is a lot more than just 3 movies. So I will just try my best here.


1. The Departed

Well, the acting in this movie is on another level. Just the names of the stars can hook you well. Leo, Jack, but most notably in my opinion was Mark whalberg.


2. The Prestige

This one is one of my favorites simply because of the twist at the end. I felt like Nolan is grinningly saying"Gotcha" very loudly.


3. The Bourne Supremacy

Action Action and more Action. A movie that can make you gasp so many times. Had fun really watching it.
